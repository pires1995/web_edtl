ELETRISIDADE DE TIMOR LESTE, EMPRESA PUBLIKA

Background

EDTL, E.P. is Timor-Lesteâ€™s State-Owned Company in Electricity and Energy Sector. EDTL, E.P. was established as a State-Owned Enterprise (SOE) based on the Decree Law No. 29/2020, of 22 July 2020. The Company became fully operation in January 2021. The objectives of the EDTL, E.P. is to create a set of restructuring measures in reforming the electricity sector aimed at expanding the supply of electricity and modernizing the National Electricity System. This also includes implementing a management system with the goal of improving the public service provided by EDTL, raising standard and quality of power supply and its reliability at the affordable prices, considering the feasibility and sustainability of its operation.

Vision and Mission

Mission
Mission is to provide its customers with safe, reliable, sustainable, and affordable electricity service.

Its Vision is to:
(a) Put people first.
(b) Pursue excellence.
(c) Embrace change; and
(d) Serve the people of Timor-Leste.

Its Values are:
(a) Ensuring Financial Sustainability.
(b) Consistently Delivering Quality Services.
(c) Promoting Economic Growth.
(d) Extending and improving grid electricity supply to all people in the country; and
(e) Considering future generations.

Objective

EDTL objectives, such as improving reliability, reducing losses, increasing collections, introducing lower-cost RE generation, and expanding electricity access to 100% of the population
